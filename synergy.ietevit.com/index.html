Xbee Communication Workshop

Xbee Communication workshop , conducted by TechnoXian mainly focuses on the students who are enthusiastic to learn Wireless Communication using Xbee Protocol and AVR microcontroller. Xbee transceiver module is easy to integrate and provide reliable wireless communication up to 100 meters. Xbee supports point to point and multi point networks.

GUEST LECTURES

Mr. Lohit Sahu

He is the Founder and Director at Phyzok Learning Solutions LLP, a firm that creates solutions to enhance education delivery systems in schools. Over the past three years, Phyzok has touched more than 1.5 lakh students to understand how the education delivery system can be made lean and multiple times effective. At the end of 2012, Phyzok launched their flagship product "Phyzok Flipped" that promises to revolutionise the current education system. Its launch has attracted attention of big names in education sector – CBSE has started promoting Flipped Learning, BBC covered Phyzok Flipped in their issue, and Pearson's published a case study on Phyzok in their book on entrepreneurship alongside Google and RedBus because of their seamless Bootstrapping business model. He graduated from BITS Pilani with a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering. He is also the recipient of BITSAA Global 30 under 30 award, "Star Entrepreneur Award" at the 5th Indira International Innovation Summit, “Hottest Young Entrepreneurs" for the year 2011.He is also a mentor at BITS Pilani's global course on New Venture Creation.He has been invited as a judge/speaker at the "Young India Fellowship", IIMs, national business plan competitions, etc.

Mr. Akshay Goel

Dr Akshay Goel is the Senior Vice President ,R&D at Biological E, Hyderabad, with an experience of over 20 years in the vaccine industry. He is responsible for external technology evaluation technology transfer, process development, early clinical manufacturing, process validation and process transfer to consumer manufacturing.

Mr. Sijomon

Mr Sijomon is an engineer from DRDO with an experience of over 10 years.He will be the speaker for the inauguration ceremony along with Mr. K.S Chakravarthy,Chairman IETE, Chennai Centre.

Social Initiative

IETE has undertaken a social initiative to raise funds for the Universal Higher Education Trust (UHET) through Synergy’16. UHET is a trust which provides a platform for the students, whose family income is not enough for the completion of their education, to complete their school education and college education. It would be a privilege for IETE to contribute for such a noble cause.
