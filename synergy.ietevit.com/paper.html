Paper Presentation Competition



This Synergy'16 we bring together Scientists, Academician, Field Engineers, Scholars and Students of related fields of Engineering and Technology.

We will entertain papers from the following branches will be entertained:

  • ECE/EEE
  • CSE/IT
  • Mechanical/Civil

Each branch will have an expert to judge the papers and to select the best the papers which will be sent to the journal for plagiarism check.



CALL FOR ABSTRACTS. (LAST DATE 31st DECEMBER, 2015)

The topics for the competition should be bound to the fields of Engineering and technology, more specifically:

  • CSE/IT
  • ECE/EEE
  • Mechanical/Civil

SEND IN YOUR ABTRACTS TO : ietevit@vit.ac.in
with subject as [college_name] [branch]

Over and above, PRIZE MONEY of up to ₹ 13,500 are to be won by the winners of the competition. Certificates of participation will be provided to all candidates.

So come and be a part of this amazing phenomenon!


Procedure


  1. Students have to present their abstract a few weeks prior to the final presentation.The selected abstracts will be presented before the judges in the final presentation. We will shortlist papers which will be published in IJRET. The participants also have a chance to win prizes worth 13,500 (including all category).
  2. A common registration fee of Rs 500 per student has to be paid.


About IJRET

International Journal of Research in Engineering and Technology, IJRET is an international peer reviewed, online and print version journal published by eSAT Publishing House for the enhancement of research in various disciplines of Engineering and Technology. The aim and scope of the journal is to provide an academic medium and an important reference for the advancement and dissemination of research results that support high-level learning, teaching and research in the fields of Engineering and Technology.
